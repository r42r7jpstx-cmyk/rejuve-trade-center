Founder

Professional Foundation & Early Career

The founder earned a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from the University of California, Berkeley in 2004, developing a foundation in analytical reasoning, structured decision-making, and institutional communication.

Early professional exposure at Citibank provided insight into global financial operations, compliance-oriented workflows, and disciplined execution environments.
