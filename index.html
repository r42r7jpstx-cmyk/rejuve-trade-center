Advancing Global Medical Aesthetics

We specialize in professional medical aesthetic technologies designed to meet the highest standards of clinical safety, treatment effectiveness, and global regulatory compliance, supporting clinics and medical institutions worldwide.

Founder & Background

The founder holds a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from the University of California, Berkeley, where a strong foundation was established in economic analysis, strategic thinking, and professional communication.

After graduation, the founder completed an internship at Citibank, gaining hands-on experience in financial markets, corporate operations, market analysis, and international banking processes.

These early professional experiences laid the groundwork for subsequent involvement in international trade, cross-border business development, and long-term strategic operations within the global medical aesthetics industry.
