About Rejuve Trade Center

Rejuve Trade Center is a United States–based international trading company specializing in the import and distribution of professional medical and aesthetic devices. We support clinics, distributors, and healthcare providers through compliant sourcing, transparent supply chains, and reliable trade execution.

Product Portfolio

Radiofrequency Skin Tightening Systems

Advanced monopolar radiofrequency systems designed for non-invasive skin tightening and contouring. These devices deliver controlled thermal energy to deeper dermal layers, stimulating collagen remodeling and improving skin firmness. Widely used in aesthetic clinics for facial and body treatments.

Microneedling & Regenerative Devices

Microneedling systems and PRP-related devices commonly used for skin rejuvenation, anti-aging treatments, and tissue repair. These minimally invasive technologies are widely adopted in aesthetic and dermatology practices.

Dental & Oral Aesthetic Equipment

Dental aesthetic devices including teeth whitening systems and oral cosmetic equipment, supporting a growing segment within the medical aesthetics market.

Global Logistics & Trade Operations

Rejuve Trade Center operates with a strong focus on international logistics and trade coordination. We manage cross-border sourcing, shipping, and documentation processes to ensure timely delivery and regulatory alignment across international markets.

Our logistics capabilities support reliable execution, risk control, and long-term cooperation with global manufacturing partners.

Our Philosophy

We prioritize safety, regulatory compliance, and long-term value creation. Market trends increasingly favor minimally invasive, non-invasive, efficient, and portable devices. Our sourcing strategy aligns with these developments while maintaining strict FDA compliance.
