Company Overview

Rejuve Trade Center (回春贸易中心) is a Dallas-based trading company specializing in the import and distribution of medical and aesthetic devices for clinics, healthcare providers, and professional beauty institutions across the United States.

We focus on compliant, non-invasive, and technology-driven equipment that meets current market demand for safety, efficiency, and long-term clinical value.

Product Focus

Medical Devices

Diagnostic and monitoring equipment including digital blood pressure monitors, glucose meters, ECG systems, oxygen concentrators, and respiratory support devices for home and clinical use.

Aesthetic & Beauty Devices

Laser systems, RF and ultrasound devices, microneedling and PRP equipment, IPL technology, and non-invasive body contouring solutions.

Imaging & Treatment

Ultrasound, microwave, and radiofrequency systems designed for skin tightening, therapeutic treatment, and professional clinical applications.

Compliance & Quality

Compliance is a core principle of Rejuve Trade Center. We work closely with manufacturing partners to ensure all imported devices align with FDA requirements and applicable US regulatory standards.

Our approach emphasizes risk control, documentation integrity, and long-term regulatory sustainability.
