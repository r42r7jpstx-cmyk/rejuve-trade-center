Founder & Professional Background

The founder earned a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from the University of California, Berkeley in 2004, establishing a strong foundation in economic analysis, structured thinking, and professional communication.

Following graduation, the founder completed an internship at Citibank, gaining exposure to global financial operations, market analysis, and institutional workflows.

In 2007, the founder returned to Shanghai and joined Shanghai Huayi Group, focusing on project management and market-oriented operations.

This experience formed a disciplined approach to execution, risk control, and cross-border coordination that continues to shape Rejuve’s operational philosophy today.
