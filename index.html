Company Overview

Structured Medical Trade With Long-Term Orientation

Rejuve Trade Center operates across medical aesthetics, clinical technology, and international trade. We focus on structured product classification, regulatory alignment, and operational execution rather than short-term distribution or promotional activity.

Our role is to support professional institutions by delivering systems that have been reviewed, contextualized, and deployed within compliant and sustainable operational frameworks.

Founder

Professional Background & Execution Discipline

The founder earned a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from the University of California, Berkeley in 2004, establishing a foundation in analytical thinking, structured decision-making, and professional communication.

Early professional experience at Citibank provided exposure to global financial operations, institutional workflows, and disciplined execution environments.

In 2007, the founder joined Shanghai Huayi Group, focusing on project coordination, cross-functional collaboration, and market-oriented execution within large organizational structures.

This experience continues to shape Rejuve’s operational philosophy, emphasizing risk control, long-term coordination, and sustainable cross-border development.
