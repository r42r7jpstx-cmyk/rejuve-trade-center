About Us

Rejuve Trade Center is a United States–based trading company specializing in the import and distribution of professional medical and aesthetic devices. We work with manufacturers and suppliers worldwide to deliver compliant, reliable, and clinically relevant technologies to healthcare providers.

Our operations are built on regulatory awareness, supply chain transparency, and a long-term commitment to quality. We serve clinics, distributors, and medical institutions seeking dependable equipment for modern healthcare and aesthetic applications.

Product Categories

Medical Devices

Diagnostic and monitoring equipment including digital blood pressure monitors, glucose testing systems, ECG devices, oxygen concentrators, and respiratory support solutions for clinical use.

Aesthetic & Beauty Devices

Non-invasive and minimally invasive aesthetic technologies such as laser systems, radiofrequency devices, IPL platforms, microneedling equipment, and body contouring solutions.

Imaging & Treatment Systems

Ultrasound, microwave, and radiofrequency-based systems designed for skin tightening, therapeutic treatments, and professional clinical applications.

Our Philosophy

We believe that medical and aesthetic technologies must be safe, compliant, and purpose-driven. Our role as an importer is not only to move products across borders, but to ensure that every device entering the market meets regulatory standards and real clinical needs.

Rejuve Trade Center prioritizes long-term partnerships over short-term volume. We focus on responsible sourcing, clear documentation, and sustainable business practices that support both suppliers and healthcare providers.

Compliance & Quality

Compliance is central to our operations. We coordinate closely with manufacturers to ensure all imported devices align with FDA requirements and applicable US regulations. Our quality approach emphasizes documentation, traceability, and ongoing regulatory awareness.
