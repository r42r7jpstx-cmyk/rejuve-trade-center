About Rejuve Trade Center

Rejuve Trade Center is a United States–based international trading company specializing in the import and distribution of professional medical aesthetic devices. We support aesthetic clinics, distributors, and medical beauty institutions through compliant sourcing, transparent supply chains, and reliable trade execution.

Product Portfolio

Radiofrequency Skin Tightening Systems

Advanced monopolar radiofrequency systems designed for non-invasive skin tightening and contouring. These devices deliver controlled thermal energy to deeper dermal layers, stimulating collagen remodeling and improving skin firmness.

Microneedling & Regenerative Devices

Microneedling systems and PRP-related devices commonly used for skin rejuvenation, anti-aging treatments, and tissue repair in professional aesthetic clinics.

Dental & Oral Aesthetic Equipment

Dental aesthetic devices including teeth whitening systems and oral cosmetic equipment, serving a specialized segment of the medical aesthetics market.

Aesthetic Diagnostic & Skin Analysis Systems

Skin analysis and diagnostic systems designed for aesthetic and dermatology applications, supporting facial assessment, treatment planning, and personalized aesthetic solutions.

Post-Treatment Care & Recovery Devices

Supportive devices used for post-treatment care and recovery following non-invasive and minimally invasive aesthetic procedures, including oxygen therapy and comfort-support equipment.

Laser & Energy-Based Aesthetic Platforms

Laser, IPL, and energy-based aesthetic platforms used for hair removal, pigmentation treatment, skin rejuvenation, and vascular applications in professional medical beauty environments.

Global Logistics & Trade Operations

Rejuve Trade Center maintains strong international logistics and trade coordination capabilities, managing cross-border sourcing, shipping, and documentation processes. Our operations emphasize efficiency, compliance, and long-term cooperation with global manufacturing partners.

Our Philosophy

We prioritize safety, regulatory compliance, and long-term value creation. Market demand increasingly favors minimally invasive, non-invasive, and clinically effective aesthetic technologies. Our sourcing strategy aligns with these trends while maintaining strict FDA compliance.
