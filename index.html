Founder & Professional Background

The founder earned a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from the University of California, Berkeley in 2004. During academic studies, a solid foundation was established in economic theory, analytical thinking, and structured problem-solving, supported by active participation in academic and social initiatives.

Following graduation, the founder completed an internship at Citibank, gaining first-hand exposure to global financial operations, market analysis, financial reporting, and client-oriented services. This experience provided practical insight into international banking systems and institutional workflows.

In 2007, the founder returned to Shanghai and joined Shanghai Huayi Group, focusing on project management and market-oriented operations. Key responsibilities included market research, client development, project coordination, progress tracking, cross-functional communication, risk management, documentation control, and quality assurance.

This combination of international education, financial industry exposure, and hands-on project management experience laid a strong foundation for long-term engagement in international trade, commercial operations, and cross-border business development.
