About Rejuve Trade Center

Rejuve Trade Center is a United States–based international trading company specializing in the import and distribution of professional medical and aesthetic devices. We support clinics, distributors, and healthcare providers by sourcing compliant, reliable, and clinically relevant equipment from global manufacturers.

Our operations emphasize regulatory awareness, transparent supply chains, and practical market execution. We focus on long-term value rather than short-term volume, ensuring stability for both suppliers and customers.

Product Portfolio

Medical & Diagnostic Devices

Diagnostic and monitoring equipment including digital blood pressure monitors, glucose meters, ECG systems, pulse oximeters, and home-care monitoring solutions widely used in clinics and outpatient settings.

Aesthetic Laser & Energy-Based Devices

Laser systems for hair removal, pigmentation treatment, and skin rejuvenation, as well as IPL and energy-based platforms with sustained demand in aesthetic markets.

Imaging & Treatment Systems

Ultrasound, microwave, and radiofrequency systems designed for skin tightening, body contouring, and therapeutic applications in professional clinical environments.

Microneedling & Regenerative Devices

Microneedling systems and PRP-related devices widely used for anti-aging, skin repair, and minimally invasive aesthetic treatments.

Respiratory & Life Support Equipment

Ventilators, oxygen concentrators, and portable oxygen solutions supporting respiratory care, home healthcare, and increased global health awareness.

Dental & Oral Aesthetic Equipment

Teeth whitening systems and oral aesthetic devices serving a growing segment within the broader medical beauty market.

Our Philosophy

We believe medical and aesthetic devices must be safe, compliant, and aligned with real clinical needs. As an importer, our responsibility extends beyond logistics — it includes regulatory coordination, documentation integrity, and responsible market entry.

Market demand increasingly favors minimally invasive, non-invasive, efficient, and portable technologies. We align our sourcing strategy with these trends while maintaining strict compliance with US regulatory standards.

Compliance & Regulatory Standards

Compliance is central to Rejuve Trade Center’s operations. We work closely with manufacturing partners to ensure imported devices meet FDA requirements and applicable US regulations. Our processes emphasize traceability, risk awareness, and long-term regulatory alignment.
