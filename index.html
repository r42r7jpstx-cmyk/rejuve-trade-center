Medical Aesthetic Devices International Trading

We specialize in the international import and export of medical aesthetic equipment, connecting manufacturers and clinics through compliant, efficient, and reliable global trade operations.

Company Profile

AI Surgeon is an international trading company focused on medical aesthetic devices and related equipment. Our business covers cross-border sourcing, distribution, and regulatory coordination for professional medical and aesthetic markets.

We work closely with manufacturers, distributors, and medical institutions to ensure that products meet applicable regulatory standards and commercial requirements in different jurisdictions.

Our approach emphasizes long-term partnerships, transparent processes, and risk-aware international trade execution.

Founder & CEO

The founder holds a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from the University of California, Berkeley, with formal training in economic analysis, finance, and international markets.

Early professional experience includes a finance-related internship at Citibank, where the founder gained practical exposure to global banking operations, financial reporting, and market analysis.

With continued involvement in international trade and commercial operations, the founder developed a strong understanding of cross-border transactions, risk management, and regulatory considerations — forming the foundation for the company’s focus on compliant and sustainable medical device trade.
