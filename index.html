Advancing Global Medical Aesthetics

Rejuve Trade Center focuses on professional medical aesthetic technologies, delivering solutions that balance clinical safety, treatment effectiveness, and global regulatory compliance for clinics and medical institutions worldwide.

Founder & Professional Background

The founder earned a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from the University of California, Berkeley in 2004. During academic studies, a solid foundation was established in economic theory, analytical thinking, and structured problem-solving, complemented by active participation in academic and social initiatives that strengthened communication and collaboration skills.

Following graduation, the founder completed an internship at Citibank, gaining first-hand exposure to the operations of the global financial industry. This experience involved market analysis, financial reporting, and client-oriented services, as well as supporting teams in data organization, analysis, and project execution.

In 2007, the founder returned to Shanghai and joined Shanghai Huayi Group, where responsibilities focused on project management and market-oriented operations. Key areas of work included market research, client development, project progress tracking, cross-functional coordination, stakeholder communication, risk management, documentation control, and quality assurance across multiple projects.

This combination of international education, financial industry exposure, and hands-on project management experience laid a strong foundation for long-term engagement in international trade, commercial operations, and cross-border business development.
