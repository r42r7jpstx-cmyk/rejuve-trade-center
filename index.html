Founder & Background

The founder earned a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from the University of California, Berkeley. During academic years, a strong foundation was built in economic analysis, strategic thinking, and professional communication through both academic engagement and interdisciplinary activities.

Following graduation, the founder completed an internship at Citibank, gaining hands-on exposure to financial markets, corporate operations, market analysis, and client-focused financial services. This experience provided practical insight into international finance, data-driven decision-making, and institutional workflows.

These early experiences formed a solid foundation for subsequent involvement in international trade, commercial operations, and cross-border business development within the medical and aesthetic industries.
