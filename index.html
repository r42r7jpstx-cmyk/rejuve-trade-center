Rejuve Trade Center

A U.S.-based medical aesthetics and clinical technology organization focused on long-term institutional cooperation, regulatory alignment, and structured cross-border execution rather than short-term distribution or promotional activity.

Company Overview

Structured Medical Trade With Long-Term Orientation

Rejuve Trade Center operates across medical aesthetics, clinical technology, and international medical trade. The organization focuses on structured product classification, regulatory pathway alignment, and long-term operational execution rather than transactional distribution models.

Our work emphasizes institutional compatibility, lifecycle responsibility, and sustainable deployment frameworks, supporting professional clinics, hospitals, and medical groups operating in regulated environments.

Founder

Professional Foundation & Early Career

The founder earned a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from the University of California, Berkeley in 2004, developing a foundation in analytical reasoning, structured decision-making, and institutional communication.

Early professional exposure at Citibank provided insight into global financial operations, compliance-oriented workflows, and disciplined execution environments.

Industrial Experience & Operational Perspective

In 2007, the founder joined Shanghai Huayi Group, participating in large-scale industrial and cross-functional project coordination within complex organizational structures.

This experience shaped Rejuve’s emphasis on risk control, long-term planning, and cross-border operational sustainability across regulated industries.

Selected Clinical Collaborations

International Professional Engagements

Rejuve Trade Center engages in long-term clinical and professional exchange with international medical institutions. These collaborations emphasize shared clinical standards, structured medical practice, and sustainable professional cooperation.

ID Plastic Surgery Hospital · South Korea

ID Plastic Surgery Hospital is one of Asia’s most recognized large-scale plastic surgery hospitals, known for its multidisciplinary clinical teams, structured surgical workflows, and high-volume institutional medical operations. The collaboration focuses on clinical exchange, procedural understanding, and institutional-level medical aesthetics practice.

Obagi Skin Health Institute · United States

Founded by Dr. Zein Obagi, the Obagi Skin Health Institute represents a globally influential authority in dermatological science and skin health. Engagements with the institute emphasize evidence-based skin treatment philosophy, dermatological education, and long-term professional dialogue within regulated medical frameworks.

The Private Clinic · United Kingdom

The Private Clinic is a leading private medical and aesthetic clinic group in the United Kingdom, operating under regulated clinical standards. Professional interaction focuses on private healthcare delivery models, patient-centered clinical management, and compliant aesthetic medical practice in the UK market.
